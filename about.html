About Bristol Community Events

A platform created to support tourism, community engagement and smart event planning across Bristol.

Our mission

BCE exists to make Bristol easier to experience by bringing all local and citywide events into one modern system. The goal is to support visitors, attendees and organisers with a simple, reliable experience.

From exhibitions and religious gatherings to musical performances and sports competitions, BCE helps connect people with exciting opportunities throughout the city.

How BCE supports Bristol

  • Promotes tourism and local culture
  • Gives visitors one place to discover events
  • Helps organisers manage bookings and payments
  • Encourages community participation and attendance
